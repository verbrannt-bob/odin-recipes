Pico de Gallo

Description

Everyone loves vibrant pico de gallo made with fresh, seasonal ingredients. Believe it or not, you can make restaurant-worthy pico de gallo in the comfort of your own home with this top-rated recipe.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Lime
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cube the tomatoes
  2. Finely cut the Onions, Garlic and Cilantro
  3. Squish the lime juice
  4. Mix all ingridients in a bowl
